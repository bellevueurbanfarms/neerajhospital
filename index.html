About Us

Restoring Motion, Renewing Life

Neeraj Hospital has been a pillar of orthopedic healthcare in Ellenabad, Sirsa district. We combine modern technology with personalised care, making quality orthopedic treatment accessible to all.

Modern Operation Theatres — equipped with advanced imaging and surgical instruments for precise outcomes.

Our Services

Comprehensive Orthopedic Care

From fracture management to advanced joint replacements, we provide the full spectrum of musculoskeletal care under one roof.

Fracture Management

Expert diagnosis and treatment of all types of fractures using modern fixation techniques including ORIF, nailing, and plating.

Joint Replacement Surgery

Total and partial knee, hip, and shoulder replacement using premium implants for lasting mobility and pain relief.

Arthroscopic Surgery

Minimally invasive keyhole procedures for knee, shoulder, and ankle — shorter hospital stays and faster recovery.

Spine Care

Treatment for disc herniation, spondylosis, spinal stenosis, and deformities with both surgical and non-surgical options.

Sports Injury Treatment

ACL/PCL tears, meniscus injuries, rotator cuff repairs, and all sports-related musculoskeletal conditions.

Paediatric Orthopaedics

Specialised care for children with bone and joint conditions including clubfoot, limb deformities, and growth plate injuries.

Geriatric Orthopaedics

Elderly-focused bone care including osteoporosis management, fragility fractures, and hip replacement for seniors.

Physiotherapy & Rehab

Post-operative and conservative rehabilitation with modern equipment, guided by certified physiotherapists.

Trauma & Emergency Care

24/7 emergency orthopaedic trauma services for accident victims, including complex pelvic and polytrauma cases.
